# Fresh Minute — Support

**Fresh Minute** is an iPhone app for short **outdoor micro-breaks**: pick 2–10 minutes, step outside, log the session, and keep a simple streak. Data you enter stays **on your device** (no account required in the shipping app).

## Get help

- **Email:** [quangbinhdang@gmail.com](mailto:quangbinhdang@gmail.com)

We read support mail for bugs, lost purchases (if you add IAP later), and general questions.

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. The app works without signing in.

**Where is my data stored?**  
On your iPhone, using Apple’s on-device storage (SwiftData). We do not run our own servers for your break log in the current version.

**Notifications**  
Optional reminders are **local** only (scheduled on your device). You can turn them off in iOS Settings → Fresh Minute → Notifications.

**Tracking / ads**  
We do not use third-party analytics or ad SDKs in the version described in our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).

**Delete my data**  
Delete the app to remove its on-device data, or use any in-app / system options your build provides.
