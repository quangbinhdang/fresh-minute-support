# Privacy Policy — Fresh Minute

**Last updated:** March 28, 2026  

**Contact:** quangbinhdang@gmail.com

This policy describes how the **Fresh Minute** iOS app (“the App”) handles information.

## Summary

- The App is designed to work **without an account**.  
- **Break logs and related content you create** are stored **on your device** using Apple’s frameworks (e.g. SwiftData).  
- We **do not** sell your personal information.  
- This version does **not** include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs as described in our public App Store materials.

If you change the App later (e.g. add accounts, cloud sync, or analytics), **update this policy and App Store Connect** before release.

## Information the App processes

### Information you provide

- Text or notes you choose to add when logging a break, timer-related choices, and similar **content you enter in the App**.

### Information stored on device

- **App preferences and similar settings** may be stored on your device (e.g. via User Defaults) to remember choices like notification or display preferences.

### Notifications

- If you enable reminders, the App may schedule **local notifications** on your device. These are not sent through our servers in the current version.

## What we do not do (current version)

- We do **not** require you to create an account to use core features.  
- We do **not** operate our own backend in the shipping build to store your break history.  
- We do **not** use the App to **track** you across other companies’ apps or websites for advertising (and we do not integrate third-party ad or analytics SDKs in the build described on the store).

## Apple and App Store

The App runs on **Apple’s iOS**. Apple’s own terms and privacy practices apply to the device and App Store. See Apple’s materials for how they handle App Store purchases, crash data (if you opt in to share with developers), and system services.

## Children

The App is not directed specifically at children. If you are a parent and believe a child has provided information to us through a channel we control, contact us at the email above.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy (subject to applicable law).
